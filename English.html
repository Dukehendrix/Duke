Grit: The Strongest Predictor of Success


by:
Angela Lee Duckworth

Summary
The synopsis of the video is to make it clear to the viewer that a person's IQ is not sufficient to be successful. He also needs patience and hard work, patience and self-reliance. Whether they are the best or worst students, they can still underperform on tests, exams, and quizzes. In her long teaching career, Angela Duckworth has learned that students need motivational support to help them persevere and have the right psychological perspective. The video conveys the idea that education should think beyond the word IQ. IQ isn't the only thing that makes us smarter and works harder. This video demonstrates the courage and enthusiasm of a student with a low IQ to succeed in life. Angela Duckworth shares her experiences and observations of her 7th graders. Specifically, Angela Duckworth proves in this video that nothing can succeed but perseverance, and that even the oldest dreams can become reality. She provided data to prove that more resilient children are more likely to graduate, even when compared to other benchmarks. Also in the video, she said, the more people fail, the more persistently they challenge themselves towards their dreams, as long as they have the best ideas and strongest intuition and finally test it.
